Workplace Safety Poster Series

Commissioned by management at Hydraulics SR-80 to create a series of workplace safety posters for the shop floor. The creative brief was deliberate: use graphic, visceral imagery that employees couldn't ignore. In a hydraulic shop where complacency around heavy machinery, high-pressure systems, and industrial tools can have severe consequences, traditional "clip-art and a slogan" safety posters get tuned out. These were designed to stop people in their tracks...because that's exactly what safety messaging needs to do when the stakes are real.
